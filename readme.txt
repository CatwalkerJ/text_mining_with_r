This repository contains a collection R scripts 
and other resources for teaching Digital Humanities
Methods.  They demonstrate basic text mining functions
as well as visualization methods.

The content was developed by Carl G Stahmer,
Director of Digital Scholarship at UC Davis
as a resource for students who wish to develop 
skills in Digital Humanities methods.
